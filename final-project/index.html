Pigmania: The Website All About Pigs

Hello friends and welcome to Pigmania! This is an educational website all about pigs and how cute and sweet these animals are!

Here are a few facts about pigs

  1. Pigs are very smart! Their intelligence is higher than a dog's, some primates and even young human children.
  2. Pigs are incredibly social! Pigs form close bonds with each other and other animals.
  3. Pigs are much more tolerant of colder temperatures than heat. Pigs have no sweat glands so they can't sweat. This is why you will see pigs laying and rolling around in mud to cool off!
  4. Pigs are in the Suidae family, which includes eight genera and 16 species. Among those species are wild boars, warthogs and pygmy hogs and domestic pigs. So there are many different types of pigs than the traditional pink pig we tend to think of!
